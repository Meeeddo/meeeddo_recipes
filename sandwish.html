CLUB SANDWISH


INGREDIENTS

• 1 lb. ground chicken, 1 shallot
• 1 tbsp. all-purpose flour, 2 tsp.
garlic powder
• 6 hamburger buns
• 1/2 head of iceberg lettuce
• 3 teaspoons soy sauce
• 3 cloves garlic, minced, Cornstarch


INSTRUCTIONS

In a medium bowl, mix mayonnaise, mustard, honey, and paprika; season with salt.
In a shallow bowl, spread cornflakes in an even layer. Press patties into cornflakes on both sides.
Working in batches, cook patties, flipping once, until golden brown and cooked through.
Transfer patties to a paper-towel lined plate; season with salt.
Spread honey mustard mayo on cut sides of each bun. Place lettuce on bottom bun, top with tomato and/or pickles
and a chicken patty, and close with top bun.


SHRIMPS AND PEPPER STIR-FRY

INGREDIENTS

• 1 (15-oz.) can chickpeas, 1 tbsp.
extra-virgin olive oil
• 1 red bell pepper, cut thin strips
• 1 jalapeño, 3 cloves garlic, minced
• 2 tsp. cumin seeds
• 8 ounces snow peas, trimmed
• 1/4 c. mayonnaise or
vegan mayonnaise
• 1/2 red bell pepper,
small red onion,fresh cilantro

INSTRUCTIONS

Add white onion and jalapeño and cook until the onion has fully softened and begun to turn golden, 7 to 8 minutes. Add garlic and cumin seeds and cook, stirring often, for 2 to 3 minutes, or until very fragrant and the cumin seeds begin to pop. Sprinkle over curry powder, coriander, turmeric, salt and pepper. Add chickpeas and cook, Fold in mayo, lime juice, bell pepper, red onion, and cilantro. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Top toasted bread with chickpea salad, butter lettuce.

PANEER & CHEDDAR SANDWICH

INGREDIENTS

• 1 tbsp sunflower oil
• 1 tsp mustard seeds
• ½ tsp cumin seeds
• 1 green chilli, finely chopped
• 1 medium onion, finely chopped
• 40g cheddar, grated 225g paneer
• salted butter,
for spreading and frying


INSTRUCTIONS

Heat oil in a pan & add the mustard, cumin seeds. Add the chilli and onion, and cook until the onions begin to caramelise.
Transfer the cooked chilli and onion to a large bowl, add the chives and both cheeses, then mix well.
Butter a slice of bread and spread with some
of the paneer filling, then top with another slice and press gently.
Heat 1 tsp butter in a pan and cook the sandwich over medium heat for 2-3 mins until golden
the other side (add another 1 tsp of butter to the pan if needed). Serve the sandwich straightaway.
