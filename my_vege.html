Salad Niçoise

INGREDIENTS

• 6 ounces (3-4 cups) mixed
salad greens
• 6 ounces (3-4 cups) of
chopped romaine
• 2 (11-ounce) mandarin oranges,
drained and oranges patted dry
• ½ to 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
• ½ to 1 cup pomegranate arils
(sub dried cranberries or cherries)
• ½ to 1 cup walnuts



INSTRUCTIONS

Lightly toss all the salad ingredients together in a bowl (make sure the mandarin oranges are dried well).
Whisk or blend the salad dressing ingredients together until well-combined.
Drizzle the salad dressing over the entire salad and toss to combine OR serve the dressing on the side for individual servings.

White Wine Chicken with Carrots

INGREDIENTS

• 6 skinless boneless chicken thighs
(about 1 1/2 pounds)
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground
black pepper
• 1 tablespoon olive oil
• 3 cloves garlic, minced





INSTRUCTIONS

Pat chicken thighs dry with paper towels; sprinkle with salt and pepper. Heat oil in a very large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken thighs; cook, turning halfway through, until browned, about 8 minutes. Remove from skillet and keep warm.
