EGUSI SOUP

INGREDIENTS

• Palm Oil
• Egusi (Melon seeds)
• Peppers
• Option of Vegetables or Bitterleaf
• Assorted Meats (Beef, Shaki,
Cowleg), Smoked Fish & Stockfish
• Dried Prawns




INSTRUCTIONS

Add ground melon to a bowl, add about a cup of water and mix to form a paste then set aside. Wash and chop the spinach.
Place a big pan on medium heat, add palm oil and heat for about 3 minutes, add onions
Add pepper mix, locust beans and stir
Add the egusi paste in bits to pepper. Remove the lid, use ladle to break the egusi into desired size/texture add smoked turkey, ground crayfish and crayfish, beef stock (if using) and stir to combine. Add bouillon cubes


SHRIMPS & PEPPER STIR-FRY

INGREDIENTS

• 5 – 7 peeled green bananas
(Matooke), ½ lb beef
• 3 medium sized tomatoes
• 1 onion, 9oz boiled beans
• 9 oz groundnut paste, Salt
• 2 green bell peppers, chopped
• 1 tsp curry powder, Cooking oil





INSTRUCTIONS

Peel, chop, and clean your green bananas and set them aside. Add oil to a sauce pan and sauté onions until they turn a brownish color. Add in chopped tomatoes, green bananas & stir for 1 minute. Add two cups of water, curry, green bell pepper and cook for 8 minutes
Add in the groundnut paste and beef then cook for 2.5hours

MOROCCAN MEATBALLS WITH
HERB COUSCOUS

INGREDIENTS

• 500g minced lamb, 1 onion
• 2garlic cloves, 2cm fresh ginger
• 1⁄8 teaspoon dried chili pepper
• 2teaspoons ground cumin,
200ml chicken stock
• 1teaspoon ground cinnamon,
olive oil
• 800g plum tomatoes
(2 x 400g tins)
• 200g couscous, chicken,
50g butter

INSTRUCTIONS

Put the lamb, onion, half garlic, half ginger & spices in a bowl
Mix and form into little meatballs.    Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large non-stick pan and add the meatballs in batches
Add the rest of the garlic, ginger and spices and cook for 2 minutes.
Add tomatoes, stock & season.
To make couscous, put in a bowl with the butter and some seasoning. Pour over the chicken stock Leave for 10 minutes. Stir the herbs through and serve with meatballs.
