GOCHUJANG HONEY SHRIMPS

INGREDIENTS

• 16 ounces uncooked
medium shrimp,
peeled and deveined
• garlic powder to taste
• 2 tablespoons honey, Salt to taste
• 1 tablespoon sesame oil
• 3 teaspoons soy sauce
• 3 cloves garlic, minced,
Cornstarch

INSTRUCTIONS

Whisk together chicken broth, soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, brown sugar, cornstarch, garlic powder, and crushed red pepper in a small bowl; set aside.
Heat 1 tablespoon oil, Add red pepper, yellow pepper, red onion, snow peas, and green onion.
Cook shrimp for 2 minutes, Pour shrimp and vegetables; stir constantly until sauce comes to a simmer and thickens, about 1 to 2 minutes.

SHRIMPS & PEPPER STIR-FRY

INGREDIENTS

• 1/2 cup chicken broth
• 1 red bell pepper, cut thin strips
• 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into thin
strips
• 1 red onion, cut 1/4-inch wedges
• 8 ounces snow peas, trimmed
• 1 1/2 pounds large shrimp,
peeled & deveined
• 6 green onions, chopped

INSTRUCTIONS

Pat chicken thighs dry with paper towels; sprinkle with salt and pepper. Heat oil in a very large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken thighs; cook, turning halfway through, until browned, about 8 minutes. Remove from skillet and keep warm.
